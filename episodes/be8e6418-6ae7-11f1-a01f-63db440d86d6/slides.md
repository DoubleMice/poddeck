# 4.2% 流通量：一场危险的赌局

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed">

SpaceX IPO 的 4.2% 流通量是典型 IPO（10-20%）的 **1/3 到 1/5**：

- Microsoft IPO：~15-20% 流通
- Facebook IPO：~13% 流通
- SpaceX IPO：**4.2%** ← 极限低流通

低流通 = 供给稀缺 → 价格极易被推高。但也是**双刃剑**。

<div class="p-3 mt-3 bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 rounded text-xs">
<strong>稀缺性即将逆转</strong>：Haseeb 直接点出——"it's very clear that the scarcity dynamics of the stock are about to flip very soon. In like two months."
</div>

# 90天解锁炸弹：史上最激进解锁计划

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed space-y-3 mt-3">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

**典型IPO锁定**：VC、创始人和老股东 **180天（6个月）不得出售**。这是市场惯例，给股价时间稳定。

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

**SpaceX解锁**：7月底首批解锁（IPO后约90天），**20%的锁定股票可出售**，流通量将在短时间内 **5x 跳增**。

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">
**好消息**：Elon 和核心股东自愿锁定 **1年**，释放积极信号。"Elon's like look, they're five-xing the float in a month and a half. Of the non keyholder stock. So Elon and the keyholders locked themselves up for a year, which is nice."
</div>

# 永续合约：解锁对冲的秘密武器

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed space-y-3 mt-3">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

<strong>Tarun 的核心洞察</strong>：加密世界长期抱怨VC通过永续合约对冲解锁抛压——但在SpaceX案例中，**这反而可能更符合逻辑**。

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 text-xs">
<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>为什么SpaceX员工会做空永续合约？</strong><br>
• 资金费率相对解锁期限并不高<br>
• 90天解锁（而非180天）→ 对冲所需期限更短<br>
• "the funding rate is not that high relative to how long they have to do this"<br>
• 9月期权隐含10%的溢价 → 永续合约反而更便宜
</div>
<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">
<strong>传统金融没有准备</strong><br>
• 股权市场从未有过"连续对冲+短期限"的组合<br>
• 过去只能通过OTC结构性产品或远期合约<br>
• 永续合约第一次提供了廉价、流动的对冲路径<br>
• "I don't think the equity world is ready for this continuous hedging"
</div>
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-xs">
<strong>潜在影响</strong>：如果员工和VC大规模使用永续合约对冲，可能在解锁日创造巨大的抛售压力——"all the things crypto people always complain about... equity people are about to find out about."
</div>

# HIP3生态：一个赢家通吃的故事

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed space-y-3 mt-3">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**trade.xyz 正在吞噬一切**：不仅主导股权永续合约，还在持续复制竞争对手的成功产品——从 Venu 的 IPO 前市场到各类大宗商品。"trade.xyz itself is just kind of gobbling up everything that's working."

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 text-xs">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-gray-50 border border-gray-200">
<strong>已退出或失败的竞争者</strong><br>
• Venu — 已关闭<br>
• Perpl — 已关闭<br>
• Dream Cash — 代币激励驱动，市场份额有限
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">
<strong>为什么无人能挑战 trade.xyz？</strong><br>
• 网络效应极强：builder code 系统锁定流量<br>
• 流动性集中：同资产只有一个赢家<br>
• 第一方优势：与 Hyperliquid 深度集成
</div>

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-xs">
<strong>Haseeb 的类比</strong>：就像 Circle 和 USDC——当你足够大时，你不会创建自己的稳定币，你只会谈判获取更多利润分成。"they will just negotiate more of the fee for themselves and less goes to Hyperliquid."

# "HIP3 应该改名叫 trade"

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed space-y-3 mt-3">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

<strong>Hyperliquid 平台故事的双面性</strong>：表面上是一个蓬勃发展的 L1 生态系统，但实际只有一个赢家。Haseeb 直言不讳："HIP3 should maybe be renamed trade."

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 text-xs">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">
<strong>Hyperliquid 的独特分销模式</strong><br>
• 供应端：通过分销商（builder codes）<br>
• 需求端：几乎全部来自 trade.xyz<br>
• trade.xyz 的实际自有流量可能不到一半<br>
• 大部分流量来自 Phantom 等第三方集成
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">
<strong>初创公司的困境</strong><br>
• "pretty tough environment for startups"<br>
• 任何成功的产品都会被 trade.xyz 复制<br>
• 没有差异化空间（"within an asset, there's only one winner"）<br>
• 另类报价资产（USDH/USDe）的需求也未见起色
</div>

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-xs">
<strong>Hyper IAN 已名存实亡</strong>：大量 Hyper IAN 团队已经解散或被收购，真正的故事是 Hypercore，而非"开放平台"。
</div>

# IPO分配失败：加密世界的滑铁卢

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed space-y-3 mt-3">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

<strong>从30%散户分配到几乎归零</strong>：SpaceX 最初宣传 30% 分配给散户。但几天之内从轻度认购变成 **5倍超额认购**，预期分配从 $1 缩水到 $0.10。

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 text-xs">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-gray-50 border border-gray-200">
<strong>被完全剔除的平台</strong><br>
• Binance<br>
• Bybit<br>
• 多家加密交易平台<br>
<span class="opacity-70">底层经纪商完全未获分配，所有资金原路退回</span>
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>成功运作的路径</strong><br>
• 上市后二级市场交易<br>
• 以 $167 购买在纳斯达克交易的股票<br>
<span class="opacity-70">"that mechanic worked" — 永续合约和二级市场代币化交易本身是可行的</span>
</div>

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-xs">
<strong>核心教训</strong>：将传统IPO分配流程（银行承销团 → 客户）搬运到加密平台的信任链和合规壁垒是致命弱点。二级市场路径更可行——但那不是"参与IPO"，而是"买已上市的股票"。
</div>

# 牙医的胜利：谁真正买到了SpaceX？

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed space-y-3 mt-3">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

<strong>SpaceX 的奇怪特性</strong>：与其他热门私有公司不同，SpaceX 的 SPV（特殊目的载体）在过去10年无处不在。Tom 指出："SpaceX is actually dissimilar from a lot of the other hot private companies in that it was like the most widely shopped SPVs ever."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

<strong>"佛罗里达牙医"的胜利</strong>：业内经典 meme——"Florida dentists are like, oh yeah, I got SpaceX allocation." 10年前通过 SPV 买入 SpaceX 的普通人，在这次 IPO 中成了大赢家。

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-xs">
<strong>但要注意风险信号</strong>：Tom 回忆曾收到过"用比特币支付可以打折"的 SpaceX SPV 推销——那是2017-2018年的骗子。"This was the same people selling you cloud mining."
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-xs">
<strong>合格投资者法的辩论</strong>：Brian Armstrong 借 SpaceX IPO 呼吁重新审视合格投资者法（"accredited investor laws"）。但 Tarun 指出取消这个门槛也解决不了问题——"even if you allowed unaccredited investors to invest in private companies, they still wouldn't get access." 真正的瓶颈是供给而非规则。
</div>

</div>

# 数据中心：从Grok的失败中套利

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed space-y-3 mt-3">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

<strong>Grok 的惨败</strong>：投入巨量算力，产出却是"a piece of shit"。Tom 直言："Grok is an embarrassing piece of shit. Like the amount of resources spent... compare Grok to like the Chinese models. It is unbelievable how bad it could be given how much compute they built for it."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

<strong>天才的转手</strong>：Elon 低买高卖——在算力竞赛早期囤积 GPU 和数据中心资源，假设 Grok 会成功。失败后将数据中心**以更高价格转租**："someone has to pay you a billion dollars a month to get rent on your land and there's no new land."

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 text-xs">
<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">
<strong>核心洞察</strong><br>
xAI 的真正价值不在 AI 模型，而在于对**稀缺物理资源（土地、电力、数据中心）的垄断性控制**。从 Anthropic 到其他实验室，所有人都必须"吻戒指"才能获得算力。
</div>
<div class="p-3 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<strong>与加密的相似性</strong><br>
Haseeb 指出这是 "the closest thing to crypto that I have seen" — 就像 CZ 用 BNB 收购公司，或 SBF 用 FTT 买资产，Elon 用被高估的股票收购 Cursor（600亿美元全股票交易）。
</div>

# 600亿美元全股票收购 Cursor：加密世界的剧本

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed space-y-3 mt-3">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

<strong>一场教科书式的加密式收购</strong>：SpaceX 以 600亿美元全股票交易收购 AI 编程工具 Cursor。Haseeb 评价道："This is the crypto playbook. Like this is actually the closest thing to crypto that I have seen."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

<strong>为什么是"加密剧本"？</strong> 用被高估的代币/股票收购实体资产，而不是花现金——CZ 用 BNB 收购公司，SBF 用 FTT 买资产。"when CZ acquires companies with BNB or when Sam was using FTT to buy up companies... this is the crypto playbook."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-xs">
<strong>Blue Origin 火箭爆炸：天赐良机</strong>：节目指出 Jeff Bezos 的 Blue Origin 火箭" blew up at a better time"——"That was great marketing." 两个亿万富翁的太空对决（Amazon 也在建 Starlink 竞品）像 "James Bond movie of the two villains at the top fighting."
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-xs">
<strong>马斯克的推文风险</strong>：Elon 刚发推说 SpaceX "will hit a trillion dollars in revenue by 2030"——这可能构成盈利指引，在静默期非常危险。"That is revenue guidance in probably a quiet period or something. That's going to be tricky."
</div>

# Anthropic 的 KYC 困境：隐私政策的连锁反应

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed space-y-3 mt-3">

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-xs">
