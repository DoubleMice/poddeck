# 为什么这期值得听

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 mt-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### 从 170 次退稿到 Blizzard
作家梦碎，把手稿全扔进 dumpster；靠 EverQuest 逃避人生，结果在游戏里**找到 career、family、自我**。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### WoW 的关键设计决定
不是画面，不是剧情，是一句话：**"让 XP 往 quest 里堆"** —— 把 MMO 从定点刷怪改成被世界牵着走。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### Titan → Overwatch
Titan 七年、$83M、全员顶尖、编辑器只能用 20h/40h。砍掉一切后，Overwatch 用**同一套引擎和美术** 2.5 年出货。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

### Tracer 的诞生
Titan 的 Jumper 抽象 class → 蒸馏成 "最好那一组技能" → Arnold Tsang 说 "what if she's British?" → 她不再是**职业**，是**人**。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### 被 CFO 一句话击垮
"Overwatch 今年要做到 $X，否则我们裁 1000 人，算你头上。"—— 19 年 Blizzard 生涯的终点。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500">

### Kintsugiyama
新工作室、34 人、**optimize for control, not money**。在后院写 Notepad 起稿，用金漆补碎瓷做游戏哲学。

</div>

</div>

# 起点: 作家，一个人，和一条狗

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

**学历**：NYU 创意写作硕士。<br/>
**偶像**：Kerouac、Hemingway、Salinger、Bukowski、Orwell。<br/>
**规矩**："this is a job... rain or shine, sick or healthy, I'm gonna write for eight hours a day."

一个人搬回加州，为了一个两个月就分手的女朋友。

<div class="mt-4 p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">

一年寄出手稿，**收到 170 封退稿信**；
每封都要自己贴邮票付回邮。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm italic">

"it broke me. Like, I went into very deep and heavy depression. I drank too much."

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

**最低点的那一天**

20 卷手写笔记、个人日记、所有短篇手稿——<br/>
**全扔进 dumpster**。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm italic">

"it was like throwing in the towel for a boxer... I'm not gonna win this fight, and you need to move on from it."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm not-italic">

**他后来给年轻人的建议**：别问 *"what do you want to be"*——问 *"what do you want to do"*. 下班以后一个人在家，什么让你感到开心？那才是答案。

</div>

</div>

</div>

# 一个人，一只 Jack Russell，和 EverQuest

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

1999 年, 朋友 Vic 说: "Yeah, you gotta play it." 登入的那一刻 Jeff 被送进 <b>Norrath</b>——那只狗叫 <b>Jack</b>, 从 Kerouac 身上取的名.

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

在 EverQuest 里输入 <code>/played</code> 看累计在线时间. 三年后他看到的是:

<div class="text-3xl font-bold text-center text-blue-700 mt-2">
272 played days
</div>

<div class="text-center text-xs opacity-60">
6,500 小时 · 每天 ~6h 不间断 · 三年
</div>

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

**他在 EverQuest 里做了什么**

- halfling rogue，叫 **Tigole**
- 加入服务器顶级公会 **Legacy of Steel**
- 先当副手，最后成了 **uber guild leader**
- 写 "30 人组队去打 Nagafen / Vox" 的战术笔记

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-sm">

<b>他老婆也是在 EverQuest 里认识的</b>——那个时代所有人默认是男的, "it was a surprise if they were actually female."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 italic text-xs">

"my whole career and my family are thanks to EverQuest."

</div>

</div>

</div>

# 隐藏招聘: 六个月饭局

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**面试官之一 Chris Metzen**——<br/>
去面试的路上**停下来买烟**；<br/>
一张嘴每句话都是 "fuck" 和 "shit"；<br/>
跟 Jeff 讨论 Westfall 和龙的故事。

Jeff 从来没见过有人在面试里抽烟骂人，"it was like I'm around my people."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

招聘启事对一个 "associate quest designer" 写的要求里有一条：**希望有创意写作学位**。

Jeff: "you guys set this up for me."

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

**后来他明白过来**：

那一连串听起来随意的午餐，其实是**一次长达六个月的隐形面试**。

Blizzard 从来不从公司外招设计师——都是从 QA、客服内部转岗上来的。破例招他，他们要很确定。

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm italic">

"And I just had the fuck it moment... that job opened up. I applied with all my heart."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 text-xs opacity-60 not-italic">
2002 年 5 月入职。offer letter 上的工资：$35K/年。
</div>

</div>

</div>

# "one of us" — 2002.4.18

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-60">
Jeff 在 Blue's News 上公开宣布自己去了 Blizzard 的帖子（节选）：
</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-5 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 italic text-sm">

"This week, I accepted a position as associate game designer with Blizzard Entertainment. Specifically, I will be designing quests for World of Warcraft... I hope that my association with World of Warcraft will serve to **comfort MMORPG fans that one of us is on the other side of the fence**, looking out for the interest of the player."

</div>

<div class="mt-5 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

**他后来对这篇的自评**：

"that post actually embarrasses me when I hear it now. There's so much ego in it... it's got that 20 year old 'I don't know what I don't know'."

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm">

**Lex 的反驳**：

但必须有那种 ego 才敢写下第一句。<br/>
"when you're a gamer and you really put in the hours... you understand what makes for a compelling experience." 年纪轻的人不知道 **执行有多难**——但那个 ego 是入场券。

</div>

</div>

# Blizzard 2002: 三分之二栋楼的公司

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

Jeff 入职那天 (May 2002)，Blizzard 总人数 **不到 200 人**。

在 UC Irvine 研发园区，占了一栋楼的 3/4；隔壁是 Cisco。楼外面全是——

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-sm italic">

"everybody is wearing black T-shirts and shorts and throwing frisbees and playing Hacky Sack and on scooters and skateboards."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm not-italic">

内部结构只有两组：<br/>
**Team One** — StarCraft、Warcraft 的 RTS 老炮。<br/>
**Team Two** — "red-headed stepchild"，前两次启动都黄了，第三次要做 WoW。

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

**Jeff 入职第一天**的段子：

"You have to go get your login from **John Cash**."<br/>
— "The John Cash from **id**?"<br/>
— "You're the guy who runs **Legacy of Steel**."

Quake 的工程师 id→Verant→Sony Online，然后去了 Blizzard。两个互相 fanboy 的人握手。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">

**地板上满是垫子**：不是因为公司给福利，是因为 futon 是用来累了趴着睡的。

"posters on the wall" + "people sleeping on futons" = 这个公司像一个宿舍。

</div>

</div>

</div>

# 小团队的"大声说话"

<div class="mt-4 text-sm">

Jeff 一再强调的一条规律——

**大团队做不出 WoW / Overwatch 这种东西**。

不是因为大团队笨，是因为一旦分工，人就会开始 **"vilify" 他们不接触的人**：

<div class="mt-4 p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm italic">

"the less you interact with somebody, the more you sort of become alienated from them and vilify their point of view."

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-4 space-y-2 text-sm not-italic">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

**小团队**: 10 人决定一件事时，所有人都在屋里。"I'm not a server networking guy, but I'm in the room for that discussion."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

**大团队**: 开始流传 "the artists just don't get it." —— 一旦你说出这句话，就等于把你同事贬为次等生物。

</div>

</div>

# "Treat them like the best of the best"

<div class="mt-4">

Jeff 给 Titan → Overwatch 团队的一个"公理"——

</div>

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### 默认心态 (bad)

有人说 "I think we should do X instead of Y."<br/>
反应：**"Well, I'm a believer in Y, why are you against my idea X?"**

—— 本能是**防御**。

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### Jeff 想要的心态

"take a moment, have a deep breath, and say, **'Man, the best prop artist in the industry is suggesting something. Why don't I listen to it?'**"

—— 你招的是行业顶尖。**假设他是对的**。

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-5 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**Rob Pardo 给 Jeff 上的第一课**（在一次 WoW 会议后把他拉到一边）：

"You're a very smart designer, but you shouldn't do what you just did to those people. You should always listen to what people have to say and **try to make their ideas work**."

从此 Jeff 给自己下死命令："anytime an idea comes my way, let's try to make it work."

</div>

# Crunch: 30 小时不下机

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

**Jeff 入职第二个月**，Warcraft III 要 gold master。<br/>
有一个 cinematic 播完偶尔崩溃，一个工程师加了 log，需要一个人反复触发。

Jeff 是全公司最闲的那个菜鸟——被安排**连续 30 小时看那个 cinematic**，触发一次次崩溃。

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-sm">

"watching everybody leave at the... a trickle out. The family guys go much earlier than the single guys. And then watching everybody show up again the next morning, and they look all refreshed. And I'm just... in the same position, eyes are beet red."

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

**关于 crunch 文化他的立场很细**——

- **坚决反对**: 执行层自己 40h/周，却让下面加班。"I don't think executives who take off and work 40-hour weeks should be telling anybody to stay late."
- **完全接受**: 自己主动周末加班。"I don't think I've ever worked less than 10 hours in a day."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm italic">

"some of my fondest memories are from those WoW crunches."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-60 not-italic">
Newport Rib 和 Panda Express 给 WoW 团队的记忆烙印——现在 WoW 老员工一听 "Newport Rib" 就会抽搐。
</div>

</div>

</div>

# WoW: "the lead character is the world"

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 italic">

"Chris famously said, **'The lead character of World of Warcraft is the world.'** And I always believed that."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 text-sm not-italic">

所以 Jeff 在做 quest 的时候，做的不是"任务"，是**带你去体验这个世界**。

- Elwynn Forest 是新手村的温柔。
- Westfall 是"刚出村就被现实打一巴掌"。
- **Burning Steppes** 从一开始就被故意放在"你必须路过它才能拜访朋友"的位置——让你抬头看，看到龙和熔岩，在脑子里种下**"以后要来这里"**的种子。

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

**Horde vs Alliance**——

原来在 EverQuest 所有种族可以一起组队。Allen Adham 坚持要分两个阵营，Jeff 和 Pardo 一起反对。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm">

Allen 觉得单机玩家在 MMO 里<b>需要一个强迫性归属</b>: "whether you liked it or not, you had people on your side." 他在 WoW 内测前退休去搞对冲基金了. Pardo 接手后第一反应: <b>"Allen's a smart guy. The fact that he was fighting so hard for Horde-Alliance, we gotta do it."</b>

</div>

</div>

</div>

# WoW 的关键设计翻转

<div class="mt-4 text-sm">

**EverQuest 时代的 MMO 升级是什么**：
找一个刷怪效率最高的点，在那儿几个小时一动不动。<br/>
"that's how you would progress through EverQuest."

Jeff 和 Pat Nagle 在白板上估算 EverQuest 的 quest 数，觉得 "只要匹配上这个数就行"。

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">

**Team play test 后的 "oh shit 时刻"**:<br/>
没玩过 MMO 的同事抱怨："I ran out of quests right away." — "你们希望任务一直有？" — "对啊。"

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm italic">

"why don't we make the path of least resistance... not to be killing creatures in one place, but **overload the experience into the quests themselves**?"

</div>

# WoW 凭什么打开市场

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

**EverQuest 数量级**: 几十万玩家。<br/>
**WoW 数量级**: 几千万玩家。

Jeff 给出的关键解释：

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

"what makes an MMO **massive** is having the other people there... but the concept that we have to force you to interact with them to do anything is very off-putting to a lot of people."

WoW 允许你**一个人玩**——周围还是有几千人，但不用跟他们打招呼。

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm">

**这是一个"不像创新的创新"**——

"it doesn't seem like an innovation... it sort of created this directed gameplay that **felt optional, but really wasn't**."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">

光谱两端：<br/>
**Last of Us**：按 Triangle 蹲下——手把手教学。<br/>
**vanilla Minecraft**：把你丢进世界，**随便**。

WoW 介于其间——带你走，又不勉强你。

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-sm">

附注: Sid Meier 的名言"Games are a series of interesting choices"——Jeff 把它作为设计 Lich King 起点时的直接引用。

</div>

# 三种 fun (Sid Meier)

<div class="text-xs opacity-70">
Jeff 引用 Sid Meier 的一句话 (后来也是本期播客的开场白)——
</div>

<div class="mt-3 grid grid-cols-3 gap-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

<b>fun for the player</b><br/>
玩家觉得好玩. <b>这才是目标.</b>

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

<b>fun for the designer</b><br/>
设计师自己觉得好玩—— <b>ant farm designer</b> 的陷阱.

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

<b>fun for the computer</b><br/>
代码跑得优雅、调度均匀——但玩家感受不到.

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

<b>Jeff 补的一组 "elements of fun"</b> — 他说游戏设计至今没有一本教科书列过:

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-2 mt-3 text-xs">

<div>📈 <b>progression</b> — 投入被承认</div>
<div>🎯 <b>mastery</b> — 纯技术磨练</div>
<div>🎨 <b>creativity</b> — whirlwind barb</div>
<div>💎 <b>loot</b> — 拆箱视觉 + 音效</div>
<div>📖 <b>content</b> — 下一座山的风景</div>
<div>🧩 <b>surprise</b> — 那只 cow level</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-70">
<b>作者概括</b>: 一个玩家在一场游戏里会多次切换动机——主线推不动就去农装备, 装备也拿不动就回去刷天赋, 然后又接着主线.

# Green Hills of Stranglethorn: ant farm 翻车

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-sm">

# Blizzard Polish: QA 能打电话叫醒你

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

- 兼容性部门拥有**每一块显卡、每一块主板**

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

**破除等级**——<br/>
"just message me. Here's my home number. Call me if there's a bug. If you think we're gonna get raked over the coals on this, you gotta speak up. I don't care what the chain of command is."

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm">

Stranglethorn Vale 有个 faction token 被玩家刷爆了。GM 急得在**到处 spawn "Guardians of Blizzard"**（会一拳秒杀任何人的神级 NPC）把作弊点全圈起来，边 spawn 边给 Jeff 打座机。

Jeff 躺在床上压低声音，怕吵醒老婆。

</div>

- 一套从零起的新引擎

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

# Matchmaking: 50% 胜率的心理学

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

**玩家说的 vs 玩家想要的**——

"if you ask a gamer, 'What do you want?' They're like, 'I just want a fair match. Like, just make it even.'"

**但真实想要的**: "a match where they're slightly better than the other guy... close but then win."

这在 zero-sum 里**不可能普遍存在**.

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm">

**人类记忆不对称**: 看到 Reddit 上"六连败 matchmaker 烂透了"的帖子, Jeff 的习惯是去查那人战绩——之前往往是 **八连胜**, 没发帖。

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">

**Jeff 事后最大的后悔**:

"we put all of our eggs in you noticing if the team won or lost. And we downplayed your individual contribution as much as possible."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm italic">

# The Legend of California

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-5 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 italic text-sm">

"It's gonna feel lonelier. It's gonna feel mysterious, larger than you. **You're gonna feel small until you earn the right to feel big.**"

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm not-italic">

# AI 和游戏: 现在是 "hot mess"

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">

**现状**:<br/>
"trying to integrate it into development is mostly a **hot mess**."

ChatGPT 回答怎么让 Unreal 里的 chat widget 自动 wrap —— 回答得**自信而错, 十次里对一次**. 命中率必须提上来.

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm">

**伦理底线**:<br/>
"no one's creative work should ever be used by AI without their permission... it can't be lifting from them. That's just immoral."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

**实际小用法**:<br/>
2000 张尺寸弄错的图——让 ChatGPT 一分钟内 resize 打包. "I wasn't gonna hire an intern to do it. It didn't take a job."

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

**Jeff 最大的论断**:

"no matter how good AI gets, **never gonna draw a picture like Arnold Tsang. It's never gonna tell a story like Chris Metzen**. That human spirit is irreplaceable."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 italic text-sm">

"AI to me right now currently, it's like an interesting fever dream."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-sm not-italic">

**对未来的判断**:<br/>
"**Small studios are the future of gaming.** The big studios basically acquire the small studios for new IP and ideas."

</div>

# 游戏是一种"每次都要重造相机的电影"

<div class="text-sm opacity-70">
一个引用 (Jeff 说 "somebody much smarter than me once described it")——
</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 italic text-sm">

"making a game is like making a movie if you had to invent the camera every time, because you're kind of <b>inventing the technology of your specific game</b>."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 grid grid-cols-2 gap-4 text-sm not-italic">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

<b>每一代艺术都吃上一代的遗产</b>——

电影 = 摄影 + 声音 + fine art<br/>
游戏 = 全部上述 + <b>电影本身</b>

Jeff: "it's Katamari Damacy, the art form."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

<b>所以好游戏为什么稀有</b>——

要同时是导演、画家、作曲、工程师、系统设计师、心理学家.

还要**重新发明执行所需的技术**.

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">

<b>Jeff 的冷门观察</b>: 最被低估的两个 discipline 是 <b>audio</b> 和 <b>lighting</b>——"most of the time, we're playing without these things, and it just feels a little off and wrong."
