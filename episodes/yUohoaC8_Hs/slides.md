# Information mover = 恐龙

<div class="mt-4 p-5 text-xl italic border-l-4 border-red-500 bg-red-50">
"The information mover is essentially going to become a dinosaur."
</div>

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-2 gap-4 text-sm">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-gray-50 border-l-4 border-gray-400">

### 什么是 information mover
三年前一个 PM 的典型一天: "让我把团队给我的材料重新 frame, 这样我老板再 frame 给他老板的老板。"

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### 为什么注定出局
这些环节正是 AI 最先蚕食的部分 — agent 可以直达 ground truth, 中间层不再需要人肉加工。

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">
Nikhyl 的一个补充: 过去这种岗位带来的正是"responsibility without authority — the greatest form of workplace stress"。 换句话说: 消失的这一半 PM 人群, 其实也并不快乐。
</div>

# ZIRP 时代的 PM vs 今天的 PM

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div class="p-5 rounded border-l-4 border-gray-400 bg-gray-50">

### 3 年前 (ZIRP 尾声)
- 免费的钱、扩张中的组织
- "responsibility without authority"
- 拼命 frame 信息、跑审批链
- 收入高, 但过得并不开心 (Nikhyl 原判)

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded border-l-4 border-green-500 bg-green-50">

### 现在
- builders 可以"直接做东西"
- 工作里终于有"光亮起来"的满足感
- 下一站可能是 founder / CHRO / 跨职能高管
- Nikhyl: "they're just more interested in their jobs"

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

### 作者概括
这是 Nikhyl 一条反直觉论断: **过去那个高薪、充实、看起来岁月静好的 PM 黄金时代, 其实是被时间美化了的 —— 它的隐痛是 responsibility without authority。 今天的痛是新的痛, 不等于更糟。**

</div>

# "Smiling exhaustion" — 新一代 PM 的默认状态

<div class="text-sm opacity-70 mb-4">Nikhyl 被问"社区里大家过得比以前好吗?"的回答。</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-6 text-xl italic border-l-4 border-purple-500 bg-purple-50">
"it's smiling exhaustion. I see in my community, everyone. And before it was just exhaustion."
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-4 mt-6 text-sm">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500">

### 过去的 exhaustion
会议、spin、approval loop, 累但没成就感

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### 现在的 smiling exhaustion
依然累, 但带着"昨天又造了个好东西"的傻笑

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-4 text-xs opacity-60">
"So I take smiling exhaustion over exhaustion. But the pace is relentless. I don't have anything to sugarcoat that point." — Nikhyl

# 为什么 builders 在狂欢

<div class="mt-2 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 italic text-sm">
"The builders are going to have the time of their lives, but if you don't love building stuff, you're in trouble."
</div>

<div class="mt-4 text-sm space-y-2">

<div>**1. 不再依赖别人**: 想法 → 原型, 中间不需要 design / eng backlog。</div>

<div>**2. 机会前所未有地多**: CEO、CHRO、founder 都对 PM 背景开放。</div>

<div>**3. 幸福曲线反转**: 过去 PM 几乎没有"把东西修好了"的成就感, 现在有了。</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-xs">
Nikhyl 的个人类比: 以前做 PM 会中途去"换家里坏了的灯泡", 因为那是"唯一能看到一件事从 broken 变 working 的瞬间"。 现在产品工作本身就能提供这种瞬间。
</div>

# Judgment 成为第一 KPI

当 test 成本降到 1/10、1/100, 会有 10-100 倍的 change 涌入产品。 这时真正稀缺的是判断力。

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm italic">
"When things are changing that rapidly, that judgment piece becomes like pretty much paramount."
</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-sm">
Nikhyl 对 "judgment" 的三个拆解:
</div>

<div class="mt-2 text-xs space-y-1 opacity-85">

- 这个改动**是好是坏**
- 这个改动**对 brand / 可维护性**意味着什么
- 面对用户请求, **不能做 100 个定制版**, 必须决定平台怎么演化

</div>

# Logo 贬值: 从"你在哪工作过"到"你现在怎么工作"

过去 10 年的共识: 大厂 logo 是硬通货, 面试官会假设"这人见过那部电影"。 现在情况在反转。

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-sm italic">
"What if the established brands is very much working in a way that's not current? You worked there for six years, you come out and it feels like you're in a totally different world."
</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-80">
面试里真正被问的不再是"5 年前你 ship 了什么", 而是:
</div>

<div class="mt-2 text-xs opacity-85 space-y-1">

- "你现在在用什么工具?"
- "给你一个场景, 你怎么判断?"
- "你觉得应该怎么想这件事?"

</div>

::right::

<div class="flex justify-center items-center h-full pl-4">

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">

### 旧时代名片
- Meta 6 年
- Google 搜索团队
- 做过一个大平台
- "Well, impressive, but..."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-5 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

### 新时代名片
- 上周自己写了一个 agent
- 用 Claude 自动化了每周 review
- 对 LLM 的行为有明确观点
- "hmm, tell me more"

</div>

# "Stop working on the car, work on the factory" — 反转了

<div class="text-sm opacity-70 mb-4">Nikhyl 指出的一条 PM 行业基因级别的反转。</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-4 mt-4">

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-gray-50 border-l-4 border-gray-500">

### 过去 20 年 PM 的成熟路径
"Leverage, scale, organizing, streamline, empowering, enabling. Don't do the build yourself. Get others to do it. Stop working on the car, work on the factory."

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### 现在被翻了过来
"What if it turns out the scale can be done very differently and all we care about is your opinion and what you build. That's very jarring and that's what we're living through right now."

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">
**作者概括**: 你用十年学会"不要亲自干活", 现在又被要求"亲自干活"。 这不是技能退化, 是 leverage 的底层介质换了 —— 从"人 via meetings"变成"代码 via agents"。
</div>

# Power years 的"disappointment algorithm"

30-40 岁是 PM 的 power years —— 你终于知道自己在做什么, 但也正是你负担最多的年纪。

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm italic">
"Your goal when you're in your power years is to equally disappoint everyone in your life."
</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-80">
手里 6-12 小时, 眼前 20 小时的需求。 Nikhyl 描述大多数中生代 PM 的心智策略就是:
</div>

<div class="mt-2 text-xs opacity-85">
"I am going to equally disappoint everyone. My parents will not be more disappointed than my kids, than my health, than my family, than my partner..."
</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-xs">
然后还要多挤一份时间出来 <strong>reinvent 自己</strong> —— 这就是为什么改变这么难。
</div>

# 过门槛的方式: 找到第一次 "joy moment"

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm italic">
"Everyone has a story and it's always different, but it's super personal."
</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-80">
典型剧本:
</div>

<div class="mt-2 text-xs space-y-1 opacity-85">

- "然后就上瘾了"

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-xs">
"And joy is the biggest antidote to burnout." — Nikhyl
</div>

# Joy, 不是奖励 — 是策略

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-4 mt-6">

### 旧 PM 工作的色卡

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### 转 build 模式后的色卡

# 给 PM 的 4 条具体动作

<div class="text-sm opacity-70 mb-4">Nikhyl 直接回答"我现在该做什么"的一组建议。</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-4 mt-4">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### 1 · 找到 obsoletion 的起点
挑一个自己不喜欢做的任务, 今天就试着把它交给 agent。 这是"engineering mindset"的起点, 也是最快的 joy-moment 触发器。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### 2 · Increase pace
"The next two years requires a lot of fire in the belly." 把自己在"新岗位第一年"时的强度找回来, 无论你是不是真的换了岗。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

### 4 · 用 skip 视角思考

</div>

# Alignment 不会死, 只是不再有 theatrics
