# 流失的数学

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">

Jason 的关键洞察 ——

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">

**cancellations 会自动随规模增长**
5% 是百分比，是指数。公司翻 3 倍，流失绝对数量也翻 3 倍。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-gray-50 border-l-4 border-gray-500 text-sm">

**marketing 不会**
"Adwords delivers the same number of leads... it does not care how big you are these efforts."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**所以有一个硬天花板：**
$$\text{MAX} = \frac{\text{new customers / mo}}{\text{churn rate}}$$

</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-sm opacity-70">
100 新客 / 月，5% 流失 → 2,000 是你能到的极限。
</div>

# 永远不要相信 "too expensive"

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-4">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

"It's too expensive" 在流失调研里**经常是第一名**。Jason 说：

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

**"that is never ever ever the reason."**
因为他们看过首页、看过定价页，然后选择了购买——他们已经用行动投过票了：不太贵。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500">

"lost budget" ≠ "too expensive"。这是两码事。

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded border-l-4 border-gray-400 bg-gray-50">

<div class="text-sm opacity-60 mb-2">Jason 在 Smart Bear 的一次实验</div>

挂一个流失原因下拉，选项按固定顺序 —— 第一个选项压倒性胜出。

<div class="mt-3 text-sm">随机化顺序以后：</div>

<div class="mt-2 p-3 bg-white rounded italic">

"all the items were picked equally like, oh, right, it's complete noise."

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-60">
Smart Bear 的真实发现：流失调研的下拉选项基本是噪音。
</div>

</div>

</div>

# Approximate cause ≠ real cause

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">

Jason 借用医学里的一个术语——
**approximate cause**（近因）。

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

"too expensive" 就像"呼吸停止" —— 这确实是死亡的原因，但不是**真正**的原因。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">

**Jason 的离题观点：**
"I hate the idea of a root cause. Complex systems do not have one root cause."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

所以别想"the root cause"，想"rooter causes" —— 一组可以早点检测、早点改的点。

</div>

# 关于 AI 帮你总结流失

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-70">Jason 对"AI 帮我跑 survey"的一个冷静观点——</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

<div class="font-bold mb-2">AI 擅长：主题、摘要</div>

"the LLM is an averaging machine, right? It's predicting the most likely."

平均值的问题 → LLM 很合适。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

<div class="font-bold mb-2">AI 不擅长：细节、反直觉</div>

"when you're asking for what is interesting and not average, it's actually pretty bad at it."

触发行动的往往是**具体某个客户说的那句话**，不是主题。

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 rounded text-sm">

**Jason 的建议**：让 AI 列主题，但同时要它把每条具体原话、链接、客户 ID 都列出来。让人读细节，AI 只是分类工具。

</div>

# 救人要在他走之前

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 mt-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### 找"在麻烦里"的人

- 没上传数据
- 频繁联系 support
- 完全不联系 support
- 一周没登录

他们还能跟你说话。已经取消的人不会。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### 要看"好客户和坏客户**不一样**的地方"

常规建议："看好客户有什么共性"。
但好客户共性里有一半是**所有**客户的共性，不是信号。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### 不知道做什么 → onboarding

"almost all companies have a whole lot more cancellation in the first day, 30 days, 90 days... than the whole rest of the customer's life."

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 rounded text-sm italic">

"if you can do a little bit in the onboarding or shift the onboarding percentage a little bit, it pays off enormously in revenue and profit over time."

</div>

# Q2: 定价对吗

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">先说一个让人尴尬的数据点——</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-6 border-l-4 border-blue-500 bg-blue-50 bg-opacity-30 italic text-lg rounded">

"Your prices are way too low because you just guessed and you haven't changed them."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-sm opacity-70">
—— Patrick Campbell, 手里握着 4,200 个定价数据点。
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-8">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### 恐惧的来源

微观经济学 101 的需求曲线：价格涨，需求降。

所以："涨 10% → 掉 15% → 总体亏"。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### 真实世界里

"you raise prices and signups don't change."

—— Jason 反复观察到的现象。有时候 signups 甚至**上涨**。

</div>

# Pricing selects the market

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">

为什么教科书曲线错了？Jason 的解释：
**价格决定了谁看得上你**。

</div>

<div class="mt-4 space-y-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

**$2/月 的产品**：一家 1000 人、4 亿美元营收的公司会想——"can't be good enough. Not mature enough. Support's not going to be good enough."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**真实曲线**：一开始往上爬（进入能接受你的市场），走一段高原，然后往下。

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

**"it's more like a mesa and not a line that goes up to down"** —— 所以涨价常常让 signups 上涨。

</div>

</div>

# 一个真实的 12x 故事

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-70">Jason 认识的一位创始人，做 B 端 / 政府向工具——</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 mt-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### 起点

"I charge $300."
Jason: "$300 a month? That's not enough."
他: "**No, per year.**"

每周 1-2 个 signup。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### 实验

"just change it from per month, per year to per month."

—— 相当于价格 12x。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### 结果

**same one or two signups per week**. Nothing changed.

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-5 bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 rounded italic">

Jason 对他说：**"what you're going to do is raise prices again."**

</div>

<div class="mt-2 text-sm opacity-70">
因为 12x 没让 signups 掉 —— 你根本没碰到价格天花板。
</div>

# Double Down 的 8x 定价故事

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-70">同一个产品，两种说法——</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-4">

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-400">

### V1：省钱叙事
"Cut your AdWords cost in half."

客户原本花 $40k/月。
- 省下 $20k
- 愿意给 Double Down 多少？ $5k
- 因为他要真的**省钱**

**Double Down 赚 5k/月。**

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### V2：增长叙事
"Double the leads per month."

同一个客户，同一个产品——
- 我本来就愿意花 40k 买这么多 leads
- 翻倍？当然愿意再花 40k
- 因为老板要的是 growth，不是 savings

**Double Down 赚 40k/月 → 8x**

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 rounded italic">

"double down gets 8x the money because it gets 40k for this product not 5k for this product."

</div>

# Pricing 不只是页面上那个数字

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### 结构
per site · per usage · per seat · per month vs. per year —— 同样的钱，不同的结构。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

你卖的是"省时/省钱"还是"增长/份额"？CEO 年底想听到哪种？

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### 叙事

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

### 市场
同一个产品，给 1000 员工的公司和给 soloreneur 是两个不同的产品。

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-5 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 bg-yellow-50 bg-opacity-40 rounded italic">

"sell more of what the company values like growth. It doesn't have to be growth. It could be something else."

# Q3: 老客户在长吗（NRR）

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">

另一种对抗流失的办法：让留下的客户**买得更多**。
这就是 NRR（Net Revenue Retention）。

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

**NRR 是为数不多能和规模等比增长的正向杠杆**
流失随规模指数上涨 → 你需要另一个指数来抵消。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

**上市 SaaS 公司中位数 NRR：119%**
"over a hundred SaaS public companies and something like two of them have NR less than 100%."

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 text-sm">

**但 NRR 会撒一点谎。**
`-20% → +20% ≠ 0%`

</div>

# 创造更多价值，然后和客户分

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-70">Jason 说 NRR 的正确打开方式——</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-6 border-l-4 border-blue-500 bg-blue-50 bg-opacity-30 italic text-lg rounded">

"how do we create more value for the customer and then split that with them."

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### 顺序很重要
**先**想怎么给客户创造更多价值，**再**想我们能从里面切一小块。不是"加个 feature 好让客户多付钱"。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### 要会量化客户的价值
"actually what's really important is to measure how does the customer value this."
如果能把它做成一个数字 → 很可能就是你的 North Star。

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 rounded text-sm">

**Jen Abel 的注解（Jason 引用）**：刚用 10k 进来的客户，你很难"回头"把它涨到 100k —— "you can't just raise prices later, you're kind of stuck at that reference point."

</div>

# Q4: 渠道是不是饱和了

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">

大多数人以为渠道是 S-curve ——
先爬升、到顶、稳定。
**Jason 说：不是。**

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">

**真实形状：先 S，然后屁股下垂**
Jason 自己起的名字：**elephant curve**。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-xs">

**为什么会下垂**：audience 被饱和（看过 7 次以后不再有效）；渠道本身在衰退（杂志发行量明明每年都在涨，然后杂志倒了）；AdWords / 会议 / affiliates 都一样。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-xs">

"Do you know right now **which channels are saturated and which aren't**? If the answer is no, maybe that's because the answer is all."

</div>

# 怎么找新渠道：两个案例

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### Constant Contact 的城市巡讲

增长停了 → 他们**物理地**跑到一批城市，开 workshop 教餐厅/牙医怎么做邮件营销。

<div class="mt-3 text-sm opacity-70">
一个 $20/月的产品，开线下活动理应不划算。结果：**restarted growth**。他们还聪明地把"power user + 代理商"转成讲师。
</div>

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### HubSpot 的代理商渠道

"HubSpot famously tested selling through agencies instead of direct. It ended up being **50% of the revenue** after four or five years."

<div class="mt-3 text-sm opacity-70">
WP Engine 也一样：大量客户是通过做 WordPress 网站的代理商卖出去的。
</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 rounded text-sm italic">

"almost forces us to start taking these more drastic bets to say, well, we got to do something and that's not one of them."

</div>

# 开新渠道 / 新产品：植一只脚

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">

"new product / new channel" 听着都很危险。
Jason 用一个两脚框架处理：

</div>

<div class="mt-4 space-y-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**Foot #1 — 站在你已经强的东西上**
"plant one foot into some strength or asset that you have"

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

**Foot #2 — 另一只脚才是风险那只**
新市场、新渠道、新产品线。不要两脚同时离地。

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

**押 upside，不押 downside**
因为一只脚有锚，押错的代价比"同时换市场换产品"小一个数量级。

</div>

</div>

# Q5: 你真的需要增长吗

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-70">最后一问。Jason 说这一问最哲学——</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-6 border-l-4 border-purple-500 bg-purple-50 bg-opacity-30 italic text-lg rounded">

"if you're not growing, you're dying. Is that true or is that the kind of thing that investors use to make founders try to grow even when they shouldn't?"

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### 公司不需要永远涨
37 Signals 那种：bootstrap，founders 每年拿几百万美金 dividends，**"it's okay"**。
涨得更大，组织可能让你自己都讨厌。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### 也可以换 metric
不是非要涨营收。"you could change the word revenue and say... maximize profit instead." 有些 bootstrap 公司就这么做。

</div>

</div>

# "你在长"指的是人，不只是公司

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-70">Jason 的一个微妙 reframe——</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-6 border-l-4 border-blue-500 bg-blue-50 bg-opacity-30 italic text-lg rounded">

"what if we took it to mean you? If you are not growing, then in some sense maybe for some people **you're dying**."

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 mt-6">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

**做产品 / 创业的人**
"You got to — you're a shark and you got to go."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

**做 CPA 的人**
有客户、生活好、每天差不多——"the answer could be yes."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">

**退休后的反例**
"they retire and kind of go downhill because they don't have a purpose... it was true."

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-gray-50 border-l-4 border-gray-400 rounded text-sm">

**作者概括**：同一句话对公司、对产品、对人是三个不同的问题。做诊断的时候要拆开看。

</div>

# 增长需要有"边界"

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-70">—— 配合最后一问的注脚：</div>

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### "Growth at all costs" 的代价

如果你只盯增长，就会做**你自己都不为之骄傲的事**。

（Lenny 说 newsletter：有办法涨，但"I just wouldn't be proud of that"。）

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### Jason 的替代

"I want to grow as much as possible **within the things that I'm proud of**."

—— 早期可能做不到（你在抄别人的风格），越成熟越能 afford。

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 rounded text-sm">

**作者概括**：前四问是"怎么长"，第五问是"是否该长、以什么代价长"。前四问赢了、第五问输了，还是可能过得不快乐。

</div>

# Contrarian corner: AB testing doesn't work

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-70">Jason 的一个反直觉立场——</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-6 border-l-4 border-red-500 bg-red-50 bg-opacity-30 italic text-lg rounded">

"AB testing doesn't work very well and it doesn't work on most things."

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 mt-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### 不能测的

战略、vision、insight。
"You don't AB test whether Uber is a good idea."

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### 测了也多半是假阳性

工具准确率 95% 听着高。但你在找的是小概率效果，**误报比真阳性还多**。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500">

### 一年后回头看

"nothing's different. The conversion rates are the same as they've always been."

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-gray-50 border-l-4 border-gray-400 rounded text-sm">

Lenny 的反驳：在千万级用户的公司 AB 测试还是有用的。Jason 同意——但**"If you don't know who the psy is, it's you."**

</div>

# 所有答案的共同主线

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-70">Jason 在收尾时的总结——</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-8 border-l-4 border-blue-500 bg-blue-50 bg-opacity-30 italic text-xl rounded">

"it comes back to the customer getting value."

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-5 gap-3 mt-8 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 text-center">
你的产品<strong>承诺</strong>了正确的东西吗
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 text-center">
<strong>兑现</strong>了那个承诺吗
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 text-center">
客户 onboard 得动吗
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 text-center">
客户<strong>知道</strong>他拿到了吗
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-yellow-50 text-center">
你在<strong>衡量</strong>这件事吗
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-8 p-4 bg-gray-50 border-l-4 border-gray-400 rounded text-sm">

**作者概括**：前四问之所以能按顺序，是因为它们全是"价值的不同失败模式"。
Q1 没兑现 · Q2 没沟通对 · Q3 没扩展 · Q4 没传播 · Q5 是谁在衡量。
