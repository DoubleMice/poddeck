# 为什么这期值得听

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 mt-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### 两张桌子
Sequoia 的 CEO 俱乐部分成 **kids table**（< 100 人）和 **adults table**（> 100 人）。同一类问题，两套完全不同的解法。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### LOCK(S) 算法
Halligan 评估 CEO 的四个字母，Sequoia 又加了第五个。不只是"好听"，是他看过一堆样本后沉淀下来的 rubric。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### 一堆 hagganisms
"不要一口一口吃三明治"、"两个人浇的花必死"、"没有银弹只有铅弹"——15 年 CEO 生涯踩完坑之后的总结。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

### AI 时代的 go-to-market
销售为什么是 AI **最晚**取代的岗位。Buyer 带 avatar、website 换成 avatar——漏斗被颠倒过来。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### Hire slow / fire fast
C-level 入职 18 个月内 **至少 50% 会走**。大公司 logo 是陷阱、"all threes" 是陷阱、Mackenzie cohort 永远不工作。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500">

### 一次雪地摔车
四年前他把雪地车开下了悬崖，骨折 20 处。醒来躺在崖底，他做了一个决定——然后离开了 HubSpot。

</div>

</div>

# 面试被高估，盲 reference 被低估

<div class="mt-4 space-y-3">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

**overrate**：面试 + gut feeling  
"I've been doing this for 150 years. I still think I over ability to interview someone."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

**underrate**：high-quality blind reference  
找一个 **你认识** 且跟他共事过的人。

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**关键问题**：  
"Would you enthusiastically rehire this person for that role?"  
"Were they the top 1% of your employees?"

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

**反例识别**：reference call 时对方一上来就问"strengths and weaknesses"，说明**他已经决定了，只是来走流程**。

</div>

</div>

# Parker Conrad 的 NDA hack

<div class="mt-4">

Halligan 说这个方法是从 Parker Conrad 那里偷来的。  
对所有 sea-level 候选人（CFO、CPO、any "chief" role），Parker 会这样做——

</div>

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-3 gap-4">

<div class="p-4 rounded border-l-4 border-blue-500 bg-blue-50 bg-opacity-40">

### 1. 签 NDA
发一份公司核心文档——最新 board deck 或 board memo。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded border-l-4 border-orange-500 bg-orange-50 bg-opacity-40">

### 2. 半小时对谈
不走面试流程，就聊这份 deck。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded border-l-4 border-red-500 bg-red-50 bg-opacity-40">

### 3. 看反应
"if they're just very complimentary and it's so great, you're doing this amazing thing, it's a major red flag."

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-8 p-5 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 italic">

"he wants someone that will challenge him and not a yes person."

</div>

# "Spikier" 胜过 "全是三"

<div class="mt-4">

HubSpot 过去的标准 8 人面试流程，打分 1-4。两位候选人来面 Head of Engineering——

</div>

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### Candidate A
四个 4 / 四个 2  
**显著的强项 + 显著的弱点**

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### Candidate B
八个 3  
**没弱点，也没强点**

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-5 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 italic">

"Almost every time we hired the three out of four, like the person with the least amount of weaknesses. And we changed it."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 grid grid-cols-2 gap-4 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

**新做法**：选 spikier 的、选有明显弱点的。"hit rate at HubSpots improved."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

**另外一招**：面试组从 8 人砍到 4 人。最近招 Head of Product 就 4 个人面完。

</div>

</div>

# Mackenzie cohort，永远不工作

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 italic">

"the Mackenzie one never works. It never works. It never works."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">

Halligan 说每家公司都会经历这类 "cohort 迷信" 的阶段——

- 先是 **Mackenzie** 派来 "专家"
- 然后 **Apple** cohort，"就是答案"
- 接着 **Amazon** cohort
- 接着 **Salesforce / Google / Microsoft** cohort

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm">

HubSpot 从这些大厂招了很多人，"100% attrition rate on all those folks."

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**为什么 Mackenzie 先天不合**

"by definition, somebody who goes to work for McKenzie is very conservative in their outlook."

创始人正好相反——"skeptical of conventional wisdom"。两种气质对不上。

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">

**"impedance mismatch"**  
你 50 人的公司招一个 Microsoft 工作了 10 年的 VP，他期待你已经有一切。**没有。**

</div>

</div>

# 大家都 underrate 内部晋升

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

**外部候选人**  
✦ 面试技巧好（大公司训练过）  
✦ 资历看着漂亮  
✦ warts 藏得好

→ 你很容易 **overrate** 他们

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

✦ 你看见过他所有 warts

→ 你很容易 **underrate** 他们

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">

**Halligan 的观察**：Brian Chesky 说所有人都在过度偏向"有经验的外部高管"。Halligan 认同——但公司普遍**没有真的听进去**。

# LOCK(S) — 评估一个 CEO

<div class="mt-2 space-y-1 text-xs">

<div class="p-1.5 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**L** — Lovable：不是讨人喜欢，是 "inspire followership"。

</div>

<div class="p-1.5 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

**O** — Obsession：有 founder-market fit 吗？不喜欢"6 个月前才想到这个问题"的人。

</div>

<div class="p-1.5 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

**C** — Chip on the shoulder：Sequoia 的观察。"like a boulder on their shoulder."

</div>

<div class="p-1.5 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

**K** — Knowledgeable：deeply knowledgeable about the domain.

</div>

<div class="p-1.5 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

**S** — Student（Halligan 后加的）：新一代 CEO "like LLMs, constantly learning."

</div>

# 5-tool CEO：新物种

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">

棒球里给球员打分的维度：能打、能打远球、能跑、能接球、能传球。  
一个每项都能到 5-10 的球员叫 5-tool player——**"extremely rare"**。

</div>

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-5 gap-3 text-center">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">
<strong>code</strong>  
会写代码
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">
<strong>taste</strong>  
有审美
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm">
<strong>vision</strong>  
有方向
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">
<strong>sell</strong>  
能卖产品
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">
<strong>convince</strong>  
能号召人
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-8 p-5 rounded bg-gray-50 border-l-4 border-gray-400 italic">

"there are five tool CEOs like Brett Taylor's one. You can code, you have taste, you have vision, you can sell the product, you can convince employees like this kind of super CEO."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 text-xs opacity-60">

# 年轻 CEO 必须补的三课

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-3 gap-4">

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### 1. Feedback
<div class="mt-2 text-sm">
最难、最不自然、最拖不得。"you have to give people feedback like constantly and it's very unnatural."
</div>
<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-70">
— 不会给反馈 = 组织长不大
</div>

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### 2. BS detector
<div class="mt-2 text-sm">
"They're constantly being spun. Everyone's trying to sell to them. The org is always trying to sell to them."
</div>
<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-70">
— 这东西只能靠踩坑长出来
</div>

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### 3. Inspiration
<div class="mt-2 text-sm">
"you're Winston. You've never had to inspire anyone in your entire life. you went to school and you're a lawyer for a few years and you started this thing."
</div>
<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-70">
— 律师变 CEO，先学会激励
</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 italic">

最常见的那一门是哪门？Halligan 答得很快：**"It's that feedback thing."**

# 起步容易了，scale 却更难了

<div class="mt-6 p-6 rounded border-l-4 border-blue-500 bg-blue-50 bg-opacity-40 italic text-xl">

</div>

<div class="mt-8 grid grid-cols-3 gap-4">

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-sm">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-sm">

小时候 CVS 买牙刷，**4-5 种**。90s 年代去 Amazon，**4,000-5,000 种**。AI 之后会是下一个数量级。

# 规划周期：从 1 年 → 3 个月

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

### Then
"the planning cycles used to be a year."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-5 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

### Now
"I think the planning cycles now are 3 months long."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80 p-3 rounded bg-gray-50 border-l-4 border-gray-400">

**理由**：每周都有新的技术变化。花几个月 plan 就是在浪费时间——等 plan 做完，前提都变了。

</div>

<div class="p-5 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 italic">

</div>

<div class="mt-4 text-sm">

**对决策的影响**：HubSpot 那些 "churn 期" 几乎都是他自己造成的——有 one-way door 的决定积压在桌上。每年总会有一次他要么推门要么关门，一开门整个公司就重新跑起来。

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">

**新信条**：那种推门的节奏，要比以前快得多。

# EV > TV > ME

<div class="mt-2 p-2 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-xs">

**EV** = Enterprise Value（公司整体）/ **TV** = Team Value（你这个部门）/ **MV** = Me Value（你自己）。后来在最前面又加了 **CV** = Customer Value。

</div>

<div class="mt-2 p-2 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 text-xs">

**小公司看不出来**：前 100-150 个人，所有人都自动 solve for EV。

</div>

<div class="mt-2 p-2 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-xs">

**长大后显形**：销售 VP 只管 bookings 最大化，"service team can handle all the downstream problems I created."

</div>

**HubSpot 的刹车**：贴在墙上；eNPS 按部门算分；某部门分数从 65 掉到 30 —— "almost never actually recovered."

# Employee-centric → Customer-centric

<div class="mt-4 text-sm opacity-80">

HubSpot 早年是 **employee-centric** 做到极致——Glassdoor 最佳雇主 #1、Halligan 本人 Glassdoor 最佳 CEO #1。  
他回头看："I'm not sure that's a good thing. wanting to be liked, I don't think is a good feature of a CEO."

</div>

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-2 gap-6">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### 症状
4 小时的管理层会议，有 2 小时在谈员工事。eNPS 60 / cNPS 25。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### Halligan 的选择
"I would give up 10 points of employee net promoter score to get 10 points of customer net promoter score."

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">

**Action 1**  
管理层会议每月一次，请**customer panel** 来坐，CEO 亲自问最扎心的问题。

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">

**Action 2**  
董事会上也放 customer panel。"what do you love / what do you hate."

</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

**Action 3**  
改 comp plan：管理层不再按 revenue 发钱，改按 retention + NPS。

</div>
