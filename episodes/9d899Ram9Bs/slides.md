# Move 37 时刻：第一次它说"no answer"

<div class="text-sm opacity-70 mb-4">这个故事最有意思的不是它一开始就成功，而是它一开始失败——然后被一个简单的小技巧救回来。</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 mt-2">

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">

### Step 1 — 失败
Alex 直接把一篇 just-published 的论文问题甩给 GPT-5。

> 模型：**no answer**.

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500">

### Step 2 — Mark Chen 介入
"**Mark Chen, CRO of OpenAI**, pushed a bit harder."

让 Alex 先用一个 textbook warmup problem 给模型"prime"。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### Step 3 — 11 分钟
warmup easily solved 之后，

> "GPT-5 was able to reproduce his full result in **eleven minutes**."

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-sm">

**关键脚注**：这篇论文是在模型的 training cutoff **之后** 发布的——

> "yes, the paper was released after the model's training cutoff."

</div>

# Priming：一个值得收藏的 prompt 技巧

<div class="text-sm opacity-70 mb-4">这是 Latent Space 在脚注里专门点出来的一个"interesting prompting trick"。</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-2">

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 mb-3">

### ❌ 直接喂硬题
模型在没有上下文的情况下被一个全新研究问题砸中——

返回 "no answer"。

</div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">

### ✅ 先解一个相关 warmup
"**prime the model with a textbook warmup problem**, which it easily solved."

然后让它推到真问题上。

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="p-4 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 italic text-sm">

> "This is an interesting prompting trick. Get the model thinking along the right lines by solving an easier, but related problem."

<div class="not-italic text-xs opacity-60 mt-2">— Latent Space, 注释 2</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">

**作者概括**：和教学生一样——先给一道课本题热身，再让模型"想到正确的轨道上去"。

</div>

</div>

</div>

# 但它仍然有"重组"的味道——这恰恰是它美的地方

<div class="text-sm opacity-70 mb-4">Latent Space 的诚实之处在于：他们没有把这件事说成"AI 凭空想出来"。</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-5 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 text-base">

> "Looking at the Gluon and Graviton results, they seem in retrospect, like many results in physics and math, **like natural extensions of what we already know**. This is in fact part of what makes them **beautiful**."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-5 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500 text-base">

> "But this was a problem that **stumped experts in the domain for a year**. Although it does still have a bit of a recombinant flavor, this thing has never been done before."

</div>

<div class="mt-4 p-4 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">

**作者概括**：这些结果在事后看像"自然延伸"——但**事前**没人能做出来。这正是物理学里"漂亮的结果"的标准定义。
