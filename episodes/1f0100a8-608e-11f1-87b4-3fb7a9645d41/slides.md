# 无 KYC 市场：被低估的巨兽

<div class="mt-4 text-sm leading-relaxed">

<div class="p-4 bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 rounded mb-4">

**Mike 的核心论点**：加密世界里，不需要 KYC 的国际市场是一条完全独立的巨大赛道，它的价值常常被西方投资者低估。

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-4 mt-4">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 text-sm">
<strong>Binance 类比</strong><br><br>
"by far the biggest exchange in the world, has never been able to have a successful U.S.-based business of any real scale."<br><br>
<span class="text-xs opacity-70">全球最大交易所，从未在美国做成规模化业务——但丝毫不影响它成为第一。</span>
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 text-sm">
<strong>Tether vs Circle 框架</strong><br><br>
"it's like Tether versus Circle. That's my base case."<br><br>
<span class="text-xs opacity-70">Tether = 无 KYC 国际市场的霸主；Circle = 美国合规路线。两个平行宇宙，各自都能极有价值。</span>
</div>

</div>

</div>

# 监管双轨：两条平行赛道

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

Mike 用 Tether/Circle 的框架来看 Hyperliquid 的定位。无 KYC 市场和国际市场不仅是合规差异——它们本质上服务不同的用户群、资本池和需求。

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-2 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>无 KYC 市场（Hyperliquid 的主场）</strong><br><br>
• Binance = 全球最大，无美国规模化业务<br>
• "anything in crypto that's sort of like non-KYC or international market is really really significant in size"<br>
• Tron L1 通过服务 Tether 国际用户获得巨大价值
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">
<strong>受监管市场（潜在上行）</strong><br><br>
• 美国合规 = "unpriced potential upside"<br>
• 但目前推动 Hyperliquid 流量的用户并不期待"明年美国公民能交易 50x 合约"<br>
• 作者概括：两个市场独立增长，跨境合规是一道可选加分题，不是必答题
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-60 text-center">
作者概括：无 KYC 市场的天花板由国际用户决定，美国合规版只是额外期权
</div>

# Ethereum = IBM

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

这是 Mike 对 Ethereum 最精炼的概括：

<div class="mt-3 p-4 bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 rounded italic text-base">
"Ethereum kind of comes in and is sort of like the IBM where nobody's going to fire you for putting something on Ethereum."
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-4 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>Ethereum 的优势</strong><br><br>
• 100%  uptime — "just sort of an unbeatable metric"<br>
• 最长的 track record<br>
• 被机构信任的品牌<br>
• DeFi 龙头大多在 Ethereum 起家
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">
<strong>Ethereum 的问题</strong><br><br>
• 区块时间慢——"even starting with something as basic as the block time"<br>
• L2 碎片化——"this whole L2 situation"<br>
• TVL 是旧资本——"like really legacy things, capital that's been there"<br>
• 近 3-4 年变化速度不如 Solana
</div>

</div>

</div>

# Solana：变化速度碾压

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-3 bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 rounded mb-3 text-sm">
"the rate of change on Solana has been faster than Ethereum" — 过去 3-4 年的净新增活性，Solana 完胜。
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>Solana 的优势</strong><br><br>
• 更快 block time<br>
• 更高的吞吐量<br>
• Memecoin + DePIN + 支付 等多赛道并行增长<br>
• "real adoption phase" 中表现最活跃的链之一
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">
<strong>Solana 的短板</strong><br><br>
• "don't have the track record" — 历史不如 ETH 长<br>
• 曾有的宕机历史影响机构信任<br>
• 与 ETH 直接竞争开发者——但开发者选哪条链"anybody's guess"
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 rounded text-sm">
<strong>作者概括</strong>：ETH 赢了历史，Solana 赢了速度。在"真实采用阶段"，两条链不是在互相消灭——它们在同时吸引不同偏好的开发者和用户。最终可能像 BlackRock 同时在两条链上发产品一样，变成互补而非替代。
</div>

</div>

# TVL 是一个"遗产指标"

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-4 bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 rounded mb-3 text-base">
"a lot of what is cited as their advantages, like things like TVL, people often mention are like really legacy things — capital that's been there that people made money off being on Ethereum for many, many years ago."
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">
<strong>旧资本 vs 新资本</strong><br><br>
TVL 里有大量是多年前赚到的"存量资本"——锁在协议里不再活跃。用它衡量当前生态活力会高估 ETH。
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>Hyperliquid 的新范式</strong><br><br>
"in the same way that many people are getting rich on Hyperliquid today, through their airdrops and taking on more positions" — HYPE 在创造新的财富效应和资本流入，这才是活指标。
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-60 text-center">
作者概括：衡量 L1 价值应该看"净新增"，而不是"存量沉淀"。这解释了为什么 Mike 持有 HYPE 但零 ETH。
</div>

</div>

# 什么让一个 Token 真正有价值？

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

Mike 提出了两个核心标准：

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">
<strong>① 程序化的价值捕获</strong><br><br>
"having the mechanism for value accrual be programmatic is really, really important."<br><br>
<span class="text-xs opacity-70">Hyperliquid：97% 费用 → 自动回购 → 长期销毁。<br>不能由团队任意决定。</span>
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">
<strong>② 一致的叙事沟通</strong><br><br>
"being consistent in communications...how you tell the story to the public."<br><br>
<span class="text-xs opacity-70">不只是一次性的 whitepaper。是对投资者、质押者、建设者的持续承诺。</span>
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 rounded text-sm">
<strong>为什么自由裁量权是毒药？</strong> "this is an industry where the default is not to trust the people building the businesses. It's just the pattern says that you're going to have a higher incident of fraud, abuse, grift, sketchiness than in traditional business markets." — 任何可以被团队随意更改的机制，都会被市场打折。
</div>

</div>

# Pump 案例：从自由裁量到程序化

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

Mike 的投资组合公司 Pump 提供了一个价值捕获机制演化的真实案例：

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">
<strong>❌ 之前：自由裁量回购</strong><br><br>
• 团队可以随时决定是否回购<br>
• 市场不确定性大<br>
• 投资者不知道明天会发生什么
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>✅ 之后：程序化回购</strong><br><br>
• 锁定一年，协议收入的 50% 用于回购<br>
• "there's a credible statement that they're investing in the protocol"<br>
• 同时推出新产品（PumpGo），证明在持续投资增长
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 rounded text-sm">
<strong>关键平衡</strong>：100% 回购也不行——"you have to give people confidence that you're actually investing into the future growth of the business." 最好的机制在回购和再投资之间取得平衡。
</div>

</div>

# 僵尸链：Cardano 的警告

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-3 bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 rounded mb-3 text-sm">
"you're seeing these sort of zombie chains are finally getting shaken out." — Mike 对 Cardano 被挤出前 15 的评论。
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<strong>问题：资金从哪来？</strong><br><br>
"if it's just purely the protocol and the foundation needs to discretionary sell tokens or continuously be selling on secondary at discounts to folks to fund operations, you have this token and it just gets starts to get really messy."<br><br>
当唯一收入来源是持续抛售预挖代币 → 恶性循环。
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">
<strong>Cardano 的具体困境</strong><br><br>
• Charles Hoskinson "really wealthy"<br>
• 生态里很多人赚了大钱<br>
• 但协议本身 "seems to be underinvested in today"<br>
• 缺乏可持续的资金机制
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-60 text-center">
作者概括：Ethereum 基金会的 ETH 抛售也有类似隐忧。可持续的协议必须有手续费收入，不能只靠抛售初始分配。
</div>

# 24/7/365 · 无 KYC · 自托管

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-4 bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500 rounded mb-3 italic">
"bringing them to a place on chain where they can be traded 24/7, 365, at relatively low cost — no KYC plays a part here — the ability for folks to trade not with a centralized counterparty is a big deal."
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm text-center">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">
<strong>⏰ 24/7/365</strong><br>
传统金融市场有开市/闭市。加密永不眠——周末的伊朗战争事件驱动定价，传统市场周一才反应过来。
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">
<strong>🔓 无 KYC</strong><br>
全球任何人，无需身份认证。这在西方看起来是风险，但在全球是刚需——几十亿人无法进入传统金融体系。
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">
<strong>🔐 自托管</strong><br>
不需要信任中心化对手方。"for whatever reason they may want to do that" —— 自主权本身就是价值。
</div>

</div>

</div>

# 加密 + AI：价值会在哪一层沉淀？

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-3 bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 rounded mb-3 text-sm">
"agents are definitely going to transact more than humans at some point." — Mike 认为 AI agents 的交易量和策略复杂度将远超人类，问题是谁能捕获这些价值。
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>L1 协议层 ✅</strong><br><br>
"Solana is going to benefit from this" + Hyperliquid 的 "really direct value capture model."<br><br>
Agents 对费用价格不敏感 → 高频交易 → 费用收入飙升。<br>
"that's how these L1s are primarily going to be valued moving forward."
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">
<strong>DEX 应用层 ❓</strong><br><br>
"it's unclear that exchanges are going to be able to be differentiated in their value capture."<br><br>
过往 DEX 在价值捕获上表现不佳。是否能通过 agents 交易量翻身？"still working through."
</div>

</div>

</div>

# Frontends & Interfaces：真正的护城河

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-3 bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500 rounded mb-3 text-sm">
"where it's been clear that you can capture value is through front ends." — Mike 认为在加密 AI 时代，触达终端用户的前端界面是最扎实的价值捕获点。
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>Memecoin 时代的验证</strong><br><br>
"in the meme coin era, the front ends were able to capture up to 1% per trade, sometimes more."<br><br>
前端不是纯执行层——它是最靠近用户的界面。
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">
<strong>AI 时代的 Frontend 进化</strong><br><br>
"products like AI research labs working on financial markets that can help individuals and institutions put on really complex and performance-based trades."<br><br>
AI 研究实验室 = 新型前端，帮你执行顶级算法交易员的策略。
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 p-3 bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 rounded text-sm">
<strong>关键问题</strong>：你不仅要能执行交易，还要帮用户想策略、找到更好的流动性、更紧的价差。纯执行场所的价值会趋近于零。
</div>

</div>

# Agent Payments 是红海

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-3 bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500 rounded mb-3 text-sm">
Mike 认为 agentic payments 的赛道极其艰难——不是因为不重要，而是因为对手太强。
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-l-4 border-red-500">
<strong>🛡️ 已有玩家太快了</strong><br><br>
"the incumbents, the Visas, the Mastercards, the Stripes are moving really, really, really rapidly and aggressively to capture the value there."<br><br>
这些公司拥有已有的客户群、信任体系、风控算法——新进入者极难挑战。
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>📊 交易 vs 支付</strong><br><br>
Mike 更看好 agent × trading 而非 agent × payments。<br><br>
交易的差异化空间更大（策略、前端、数据分析），支付的差异化极难。
</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-3 text-xs opacity-60 text-center">
作者概括：加密世界的 AI 机会在"交易"和"投机"端，不在"支付"端。Visa/Mastercard 不是轻易能被颠覆的。
</div>

</div>

# Crypto VC 正在被颠覆

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-3 bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 rounded mb-3 text-sm">
"the cost to get started on something has dropped — you can hire fewer people to build to an MVP, because basically you have like all the world's developers available to you in Claude."
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm text-center">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-2 border-green-200">
<strong>💰 启动成本骤降</strong><br><br>
<div class="text-xs opacity-70">AI 编程工具让非技术人员也能做出 MVP。Pre-seed 项目数量可能暴增，但"signal vs noise"更难分辨。</div>
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-yellow-50 border-2 border-yellow-200">
<strong>📈 规模化加速</strong><br><br>
<div class="text-xs opacity-70">"the ability to scale to really big numbers has increased." Pump、OpenSea 等跑出了极度幂律的增长曲线。</div>
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-red-50 border-2 border-red-200">
<strong>⏳ 可持续性仍是难题</strong><br><br>
<div class="text-xs opacity-70">"very few multi-cycle long-duration businesses that stood the test of time." 大部分跨周期活下来的都在 DeFi。</div>
</div>

</div>

# 人才与资本：一个独特时刻

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-3 bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-500 rounded mb-3 text-sm">
"most of the world's venture dollars and private equity dollars, private capital is going into AI first companies. Anything outside of pure AI, it's somewhat capital starved." — Mike 认为非 AI 赛道正在经历资本和人才的双重真空。
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">

• 早期加密赢家带着财富和远见进入相邻行业<br>
• "Brian Armstrong just announced his multi-hundred million dollar raise for his longevity firm"
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500">
<strong>🏦 VC 也在跨界</strong><br><br>
• "most of Paradigm's recent deals seem to be in areas outside of crypto"<br>
• Framework 做公开市场 + Better.com<br>
• 加密 VC 在能源、算力、计算等赛道扩张<br>
• 但保留加密根基——"keeping their crypto roots and advantages"

# Crypto VC 的新玩法

<div class="text-sm leading-relaxed mt-2">

<div class="p-3 bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 rounded mb-3 text-sm">
Mike 描绘了加密 VC 正在经历的三个根本性变化：
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-3 mt-3 text-sm">

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-green-50 border-l-4 border-green-500">
<strong>① 更早看 traction</strong><br><br>
"looking for traction much sooner and proof points that somebody could build something that has users, that has a little bit of emergent economic activity on top of it."<br><br>
MVP 太容易做了 → 需要在更早期看到用户证据。
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-orange-50 border-l-4 border-orange-500">
<strong>② 找可持续性</strong><br><br>
经过三个应用层周期后，"what's sustainable behavior" 成为核心问题。DeFi 龙头是少数跨周期存活的案例。
</div>

<div class="p-3 rounded bg-purple-50 border-l-4 border-purple-500">
<strong>③ 区块链改良已有模式</strong><br><br>
"things that have existed in the world before, but done better because of blockchain rails."<br><br>
加密不是要发明全新事物，而是把已有模式用区块链做得更好：always on、更便宜、全球流动性、无许可。
</div>
